Method of Image Denosing Based on Partial Differential Equation

MECHENG 502 Final Project @ Umich | 2019 W

The image denoising process is converted to an optimization problem using partial differential equations and numerical method for solution. The focus of this project is mainly on image modeling and simulation. This method is based on the ROF model, which defines that the total deviation of the image is the integration of gradient magnitude. It gives an opportunity to solve this problem by converting from a denoising problem to a minimized optimization. By applying the Lagrange Multiplier and Euler-Lagrange differential equation, the optimization could be achieved by iterations. The parameters like iteration steps and Lagrange Multiplier have an effect on the final result. Therefore, attempts and adjustments finally lock the optimal parameters in this denoising problem.
